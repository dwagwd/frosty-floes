One mascot, many moods

A calm brand with a repeatable product shape.

The anchor is not “make more apps.” The anchor is a recognizable Frosty Floes feeling: cool, cute, clever, and rewarding in under ten minutes.

One hero

Keep one penguin mascot at the center so every app feels like a different outfit, not a different company.

One cadence

Every product should deliver a complete loop in roughly 30 seconds to 10 minutes, whether it is play, focus, or capture.

One visual language

Ice glass surfaces, aurora light, quiet typography, and gentle motion create a world that is instantly ownable.

The strongest shared rule is pacing.

Every app should feel complete before the ice melts.
30 seconds to start

The entry cost has to stay tiny. That is what makes casual play and light utility feel like the same ecosystem.

10 minutes to satisfaction

A short, finite reward loop becomes the brand signature across games, notes, focus, and habits.

Quiet, not empty

The mood should stay clean and soothing without drifting into sterile productivity UI or noisy toy-box design.
